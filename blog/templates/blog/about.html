Founders Message

This site was founded with intent to help. In these trying times, with COVID-19 rampaging its way across the world and riots in the streets, we wanted to offer a site where people could openly express themselves. We wanted to offer a place where you could get help when you need it. We are working with multiple hospitals to try and get as many people as possible the aid they need. We hope that you enjoy, and that we do help.

Our Founder

Theodore Bonham, or TLBones, is a small-time web developer just getting started. Previously having dealt with very difficult times and a rough time handling depressive and anxious tendencies, he sought out ways to help those who face those same difficulties. This site was his way of doing that.
